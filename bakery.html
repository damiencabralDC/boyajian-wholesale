Real-citrus flavor that survives the bake, every batch.

The Production Problem

Fresh zest doesn't scale. Synthetic flavor fades through the bake.

Zesting fresh lemons at production volume isn't realistic, and synthetic citrus loses top-note character through the oven. Infused citrus oils give R&D a way to keep a real-fruit profile in the finished crumb, at production dosing.

Where it goes

Applications across the bakery

01 · Cakes & Sweet Goods

Lemon & orange in batters and crumbs

The challenge

Citrus flavor that lasts through the bake, without the cost of fresh zest or the off-notes of synthetic flavoring.

What Boyajian does

Infused citrus oils disperse evenly in batter, hold through standard bake temperatures, and finish bright. Typical dosing: 0.1–0.3% by batter weight; we'll dial in the spec with your team.

Download application sheet
02 · Glazes & Fillings

Lemon glaze, orange filling, citrus fondant

The challenge

Cold-process applications need a flavor that disperses fully and stays bright in a high-sugar matrix without crystallization or separation.

What Boyajian does

Oil-based, water-dispersible with proper emulsification, and stable in sugar systems. Our R&D team works with bakery production teams on dosing and integration protocols.

Download application sheet
03 · Savory Bakery

Garlic oil in savory breads, crackers, and savory pastries

The challenge

Savory bakery products need garlic flavor that survives high heat without burning or going acrid, and that integrates evenly into dough systems.

What Boyajian does

Our acidification process produces a stable garlic oil that holds up in oven environments. Real garlic flavor in finished cracker, focaccia, and savory pastry.
