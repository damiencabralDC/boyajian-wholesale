Why Manufacturers Work With Us

Real ingredients. Scaled responsibly.

Most ingredient suppliers are either large industrial houses (long lead times, rigid specs, high MOQs) or small artisans (limited capacity, thin documentation). Boyajian sits between them: pilot runs from 5–25 gallons, full COA and allergen documentation per lot, commercial production scaled against your forecast.

Capabilities

Three ways we partner

01 · Bulk ingredient supply

Our standard infused oils (garlic, citrus, chili) supplied in bulk for incorporation into your product line. Drums, totes, or palletized in your spec packaging. Consistent specs, COA with every lot, predictable lead times.

02 · Custom blends

Proprietary flavor profiles developed in collaboration with your R&D team. Start from a brief, iterate with samples, lock the spec, run production. The blend stays exclusive to your product line.

03 · Private label & co-pack

Your brand on the bottle. We handle infusion, filling, labeling, and case packing for retail, foodservice, or DTC. Many of the specialty brands you'd recognize are us under the hood.

Documentation & QA

COAs, ingredient declarations, nutritional panels, allergen statements, and kosher certifications. Built-in QA process with retained samples on every lot.

Flexible run sizes

Run sizes from pilot batches for product development through to ongoing commercial volumes. Smaller MOQs than industrial suppliers, with realistic ramp-up timelines.

R&D partnership

Our team has worked on flavor profiles for some of the most recognized specialty food brands in North America. We collaborate, we don't dictate.

How an Engagement Works

From inquiry to first commercial run

01

Discovery

Brief intro call to understand your product, application, target volume, and timeline. We figure out together whether we're a fit. If not, we'll tell you.

02

Sample & Spec

Send relevant samples for in-application testing. For custom blends, iterate on the flavor profile with R&D until we land the spec.

03

Pilot Run

Small-batch production run to validate the spec at scale. Documentation, COA, and packaging signed off before commercial launch.

04

Commercial Production

Scheduled production runs against your forecast. Same recipe, same supplier, every time. Quarterly business reviews to plan ahead.
