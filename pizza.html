Where it Goes

Applications across the operation

01 · Finishing

Pizza finishing oil & crust brush

The challenge

Fresh-garlic finishing requires constant prep, has inconsistent flavor across shifts, and the oil-soaked garlic creates a food safety risk if it sits out.

What Boyajian does

A single ingredient, brushed on rims and drizzled post-bake. Same flavor every time, shelf-stable for 12 months, no prep. Dosing: 1 oz per 14" pie typical.

Download application sheet
02 · Sauces & Prep

Marinara base & spec sauces

The challenge

Commissary sauces lose garlic intensity from batch to batch, and the variance compounds when you ship to multiple units.

What Boyajian does

Spec the recipe with a fixed weight of garlic oil per batch. Pizza in store #1 tastes like pizza in store #100. Standard dosing per batch. We'll work with your R&D team to dial it in.

Download application sheet
03 · Specialties

Garlic knots, breadsticks, garlic butter

The challenge

Side items that lean on garlic flavor are often what differentiates one fast-casual pizza brand from the next, and they're the hardest to keep consistent.

What Boyajian does

Build a signature compound butter or post-bake brush with Boyajian garlic oil plus our parmesan or herb blends. Locks in the flavor profile across every unit.
