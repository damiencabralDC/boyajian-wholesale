The Innovation Problem

In premium snack, real flavor differentiates. Synthetic flavor reads thin on a panel and on the tongue.

In a category where shelf placement depends on standing out, "real ingredients" is no longer just a marketing claim. It's a baseline expectation from a more selective consumer. Boyajian gives R&D teams a tool to deliver authentic flavor in a format that works on a production line.

01 · Coatings & Seasonings

Chili oil & flavored coating systems

The challenge

Differentiating in a crowded snack category requires real-tasting heat, citrus, or savory flavor, in a format that holds up on the production line.

What Boyajian does

Real-ingredient oils incorporate into oil-based and seasoning-based coating systems. Heat and chili profiles, citrus brightness, savory depth, engineered for spray application or seasoning premix.

02 · Popcorn & Puffed Snacks

Flavored popping oils & finishing oils

The challenge

Popcorn and puffed snacks live or die on top-note delivery: there's no fat, sugar, or texture system to mask a thin flavor profile.

What Boyajian does

Concentrated, real-ingredient oils that can be added pre- or post-popping. Custom blends developed in collaboration with your team for the flavor profile that fits your brand.

Garlic oil in cracker & savory bakery snacks
