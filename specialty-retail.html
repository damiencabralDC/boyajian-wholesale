Frequently Asked

Quick answers for specialty buyers

We use a blend of pure and extra virgin olive oil, and it's a flavor decision, not a cost one. At 100% extra virgin the olive oil is so bold it fights the garlic and herbs we're infusing. Our blend lets the infused flavor come through clean, which is why about 95% of the market prefers it. We're always refining our sourcing as we find neutral oils that perform.

Pure lemon oil is pressed straight from the rind of the fruit. A 3.4oz retail bottle holds the oil of about 225 lemons. A typical extract is only 5 to 10% lemon oil, with the rest being alcohol and water. Because ours adds no water, a few drops flavors a recipe without changing the ratios, which matters in baking. The flavor is 100% natural and true to the fruit.

Yes. We don't just press whatever fruit comes in and call it that year's lemon. We hold each oil to fixed flavor specs, controlling for the components that change how an oil performs, so the bottle a customer loves tastes the same on the next order.
